How to Register an Indian Subsidiary?

1
Choose Company Name

Pick a unique name as per MCA guidelines and apply for approval online[5][6].

2
Get DIN & DSC

Obtain Digital Signature Certificates and Director Identification Numbers for all directors (at least one Indian resident)[3][5][6].

3
Draft MOA & AOA

Prepare Memorandum and Articles of Association as per Companies Act, 2013[3][5][6].

4
File Incorporation Documents

File all documents, including certified parent company documents, with ROC via MCA portal (SPICe+ form)[3][5][6][8].

5
Certificate & Compliance

Receive Certificate of Incorporation, open a bank account, and complete FDI and tax registrations[3][5][6].
